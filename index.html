Virtual Pet

My team and I designed an inter-active Virtual Pet. Complete with Robotic and Live versions of all animals. Users are able to play with and care for their pets. Walking, feeding and even cleaning up after them. Fun for the whole family.

Columbus Parks and Rec Review

In this team exercise, our team brought out the best in Columbus Parks and Recreation. This application gives the user a choice of parks from the greater Columbus area to choose from. Users are guided to their park experience by making a choice of the activities they are inteerested in. From there the computer searches the database and givesthe user reviews of the park to help them choose. They can also leave a review of a park or another review.
